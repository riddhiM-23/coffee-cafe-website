OUR STORY

A room built for the second cup.

Lumière started as a tiny roaster tucked behind a flower shop. What began with a single espresso machine grew into a gathering place for coffee lovers, creatives, and neighbors.

Every bean is sourced responsibly, every pastry is baked fresh, and every cup is crafted with care.
